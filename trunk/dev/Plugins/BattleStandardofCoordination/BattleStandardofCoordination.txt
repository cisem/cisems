WHAT DOES THE ITEM "Battle Standard of Coordination" DO?
Place a Guild Battle Standard that increases the experience, honor and reputation gain from killing monsters for all guild members that stay within 100 yards of the Battle Standard by 15%.  Lasts 15 min. (10 Min Cooldown).
