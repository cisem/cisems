WHAT DOES IT DO?
It will use "Crystal of Insanity" if you have it in your bag.

WHAT DOES THE ITEM "Crystal of Insanity" DO?
Increases all stats by 500 for 1 hour.  Counts as both a Battle and Guardian elixir. (15 Min Cooldown).

WHERE CAN I GET THE ITEM?
By killing the rare "Sulik'shor" in Valley of the Four Winds.
Or by looting Eerie Crystal on Timeless Isle.
